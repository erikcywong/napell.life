Who We Are

Napell Life is the agricultural technology division of NAPELL BIO, dedicated to applying acoustic frequency therapy to coffee cultivation.

🎯

Our Mission

To make chemical-free, sound-grown coffee the global standard —improving yield, flavor, and sustainability without synthetic inputs.

🔬

Our Method

We combine plant biology, acoustics, and machine learning to develop frequency protocols that optimize coffee plant development at every stage.

🌍

Our Impact

Active in 40+ countries, serving 1,247+ farms. Our technology has improved coffee yields while eliminating chemical inputs.
