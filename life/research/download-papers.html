2024 Journal of Agricultural Science

Acoustic Frequency Effects on Coffea arabica Growth

Demonstrates how 500Hz acoustic treatment increases germination rates by +22% and stem diameter by +14.3% through mechanosensitive ion channel activation.

Mechanotransduction in Plant Acoustic Response

Reveals the molecular mechanisms behind plant response to acoustic waves through MSL protein activation and calcium ion signaling cascades.

Flavor Enhancement Through Acoustic Treatment
