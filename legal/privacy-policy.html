Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account, use our services, or contact us. This may include:

  • Name and contact information
  • Farm location and size
  • Payment and billing information
  • Device and sensor data from your farm
  • Usage data and analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our acoustic therapy services
  • Process payments and manage your account
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Analyze farm performance and optimize frequency protocols
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Business partners with your consent

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your farm data
